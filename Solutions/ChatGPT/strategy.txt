# **📘 BUSINESS CASE DOCUMENT**

### **Post-Death Financial Asset Discovery & Claim Assistance Platform**

---

# **1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

Every year, **₹1–1.8 lakh crore** worth of financial assets (bank deposits, EPF, NPS, insurance, mutual funds, demat holdings, etc.) remain **unclaimed** in India due to:

* Sudden deaths
* Scattered financial information
* Family’s lack of awareness
* Missing documents or passwords
* No centralized asset visibility
* Entire family affected (accidents)
* Children/nominees too young or uninformed

This business case proposes a **secure, digital “Financial Legacy Vault” + “Post-Death Claim Assistance Service”**, enabling families to:

1. **Discover** all financial assets of the deceased
2. **Access** required documents & instructions
3. **Claim** assets across institutions
4. **Store & transfer** financial information only upon death

---

# **2. PROBLEM STATEMENT**

### **Primary Problem**

When a person dies unexpectedly, their family **does not know**:

* Where their money is
* What assets they owned
* How to access those assets
* Which accounts have nominees
* Which accounts have gone dormant
* Which policies/mutual funds exist
* How to initiate claims

### **Why It Happens?**

* India has fragmented financial ecosystem: banks, insurers, EPF, NPS, demat, wallets, fintech apps.
* People hold **20–50 different accounts** over a lifetime.
* No single registry for all personal assets.
* Most Indians **don’t maintain a will** (less than 15%).
* Majority avoid sharing financial details for privacy.
* Documentation is outdated, scattered, or unknown to family.

### **Major Gaps**
